Edgebug

If you land on the edge of a platform on the same tick as you slide off, you do not register as landing. This means:
  • No fall damage
  • You don't get teleported if there was a tele on the platform
  • You don't lose any horizontal speed
  • But you DO lose all vertical speed
Usually, their purpose in a map is obvious OR it's a difficult means of getting slightly more distance/slowing down in a vertical strafe jump. Most exceptions are edgesyncs in horizontal hole jumps.

Edgebugs can be useful as a setup for bounces, walkoff or crouch walkoff height (jump_crypt).

Edgebugs which happen at bounce heights also cause you to enter walking (or crouch walking), which slows your speed despite avoiding teles.
