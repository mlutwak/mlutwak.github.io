Bhop

what

Bhopping (bunnyhopping) is jumping on the same tick that you land. Note that this is a different mechanic than "bounce hopping", which is a bhop from pseudo-ground.

This conserves momentum (up to 400ups (?)), despite the Source engine's "anti bhop" mechanic of slowing you down to your base speed whenever you are in contact with the ground.

"KZ" courses/jumps refer to the goldsrc movement training maps; they usually dont allow any rocket/sticky mechanics. Examples: when b4, armature bsomething
